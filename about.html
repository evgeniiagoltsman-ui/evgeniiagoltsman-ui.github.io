Our Story

Driven by Passion, Guided by Science: The Cellora Journey

Cellora was founded by a team of visionary clinician-scientists, united by a common goal: to revolutionize immunology diagnostics. Our journey began in a small lab, fueled by an insatiable curiosity and a deep empathy for patients suffering from complex immunological disorders. We believe that by combining deep clinical insights with cutting-edge AI, we can unlock new pathways for understanding disease and accelerate the development of life-changing therapies. Our youthful energy is matched only by our rigorous scientific approach, ensuring every discovery is grounded in evidence and driven by a genuine desire to improve human health.
