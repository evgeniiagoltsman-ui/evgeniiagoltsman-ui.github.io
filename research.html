Pioneering Research in Immunological Health

Rare Immunological Disorders

Targeting the most challenging immunological conditions, we leverage AI to identify subtle genetic and proteomic signatures that elude conventional diagnostics. Our research focuses on uncovering underlying mechanisms for personalized therapeutic interventions.

Key Mechanisms:

  • Genomic sequencing for novel mutation discovery.
  • Proteomic analysis of immune cell subsets.
  • Single-cell transcriptomics to map disease progression.
  • Deep phenotyping via advanced imaging techniques.

Autoimmune Diseases

Unraveling the complexities of autoimmune pathogenesis, Cellora employs AI to discern patterns in immune dysregulation, T-cell repertoire shifts, and B-cell responses. Our goal is to predict disease flares and guide targeted immunomodulation.

Key Mechanisms:

  • Identification of pathogenic autoantibody profiles.
  • Characterization of immune checkpoint dysregulation.
  • Analysis of microbiome-immune system interactions.
  • Investigation of cellular senescence in inflammation.

Allergy Mechanisms

Addressing the growing global burden of allergies, our research uses AI to dissect IgE-mediated pathways, mast cell activation, and eosinophilic inflammation. We aim to identify novel targets for prevention and more effective management of allergic reactions.

Key Mechanisms:

  • Mapping allergen-specific IgE responses.
  • Elucidating mast cell activation and degranulation pathways.
  • Characterizing epithelial barrier dysfunction.
  • Understanding inflammatory cytokine networks in allergic responses.

Driving Innovation with Ethical AI

At Cellora, our commitment extends beyond scientific discovery to responsible innovation. We integrate stringent ethical guidelines and human-in-the-loop validation into every stage of our AI-assisted research, ensuring trustworthy and impactful diagnostics.
