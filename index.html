About Us

IMMUNO LAB brings together medical scientists and engineers to explore new frontiers in immunological diagnostics. Our goal is to translate cutting-edge research into accessible laboratory and instrumental tests that improve early detection, treatment, and monitoring of immune-related diseases.

Research Areas

  • Allergy diagnostics — exploring mechanisms of hypersensitivity
  • Autoimmune biomarkers — identifying patterns of immune dysregulation
  • Microbiome and immune response — studying the connection between gut flora and immunity
  • Novel laboratory and instrumental methods — from prototype to clinical validation
