Innovative Immunology Research

IMMUNO LAB is a startup focused on developing and validating new types of laboratory and instrumental diagnostics for patients with allergic and immunological disorders.

Learn more

About Us

Our mission is to advance the field of clinical immunology by designing innovative diagnostic methods and tools. We collaborate with medical laboratories, researchers, and biotech partners to translate scientific discoveries into practical tests that improve patient outcomes.

Research Areas

  • Allergy diagnostics — identifying immune hypersensitivity mechanisms
  • Autoimmune biomarkers — exploring immune dysregulation patterns
  • Microbiome and immune response — studying the interplay between gut flora and immunity
  • Novel laboratory and instrumental tests — from concept to validation
